Our Privacy Policy was developed as an extension of our commitment to combine the highest-quality products and services with the highest level of integrity in dealing with our clients and partners. This statement will be continuously assessed against new technologies, business practices and our customers' needs.
 

What Information Do We Collect?

Personal data means specific information about personal or material circumstances of an identified or identifiable natural person. This includes information such as the name, postal address, e-mail address or phone number. Also other data such as the IP address can represent personal data.

In general, to use the website you do not need to reveal any personal data. By using the site only access data without reference to your person (traffic and location data, statistical data about your browsing behavior) are collected. These data are processed and stored only to improve our website. Conclusions on your person can and will not be taken.

Sunstar will inform you about any collection, processing and use of personal data as well as on the scope and purpose of this collection, processing and use. Sunstar will always only collect, process or use personal data that are necessary to achieve the announced purpose. Sunstar will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent. In detail:

  • Sunstar will collect, process and use your personal data when you provide it as part of questions you direct to Sunstar voluntarily. These data are used to answer your questions and are solely for these purposes shared with third parties. After answering your questions these data will not be used anymore and will be deleted after the statutory retention periods.
  • Sunstar will collect, process and use your personal data when you provide it voluntarily to order Sunstar email-newsletters. These data will not be shared with third parties and will only be used for advertising purposes of Sunstar. Sunstar will ask for your prior and explicit approval. You can revoke this approval at any time informally and without stating any reason. Please contact: [Sunstar Suisse SA, Route de Pallatex 11, 1163 Etoy, Switzerland, contact@guidor.com]. Upon such request, we will remove your data from our database. Broadly speaking, we use personal information for purposes of administering our business activities, providing customer service and making available other products and services to our customers and prospective customers. We use Cookies on this website. Like most websites, we use cookies to make our site work properly and to provide you with the best possible experience. By continuing to use our website, you accept the use of the cookies. 

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are a feature of web browser software that allows web servers to recognize the computer used to access a web site. Cookies are small pieces of data that are stored by a user's web browser on the user's hard drive. Cookies can remember what information a user accesses on one web page to simplify subsequent interactions with that web site by the same user or to use the information to streamline the user's transactions on related web pages. This makes it easier for a user to move from web page to web page and to complete commercial transactions over the Internet. Cookies should make your online experience easier and more personalized.

How Do We Use Information We Collect from Cookies?

We use web site browser software tools such as cookies and web server logs to gather information about our web site users' browsing activities, in order to constantly improve our web site and better serve our customers. This information assists us to design and arrange our web pages in the most user-friendly manner and to continually improve our web site to better meet the needs of our customers and prospective customers. Cookies help us collect important business and technical statistics. The information in the cookies lets us trace the paths followed by users to our web site as they move from one page to another. Web server logs allow us to count how many people visit our web site and evaluate our web site's visitor capacity. We do not use these technologies to capture your individual email address or any personally identifying information about you, although they do permit us to send focused online banner advertisements or other such responses to you.

Google Analytics

Please note that afterwards you may not use the website and its features to its full extent.
